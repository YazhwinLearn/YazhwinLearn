    Overview of Entrepreneurship and Innovation

    Entrepreneurship and Innovation involve identifying opportunities, developing creative solutions, and taking risks to create value in the market. Entrepreneurs are individuals who initiate and manage business ventures, leveraging innovative ideas and resources to address unmet needs and generate profit. Innovation drives entrepreneurship by fostering creativity, experimentation, and adaptation to changing market conditions. Successful entrepreneurs embrace uncertainty, resilience, and a growth mindset, continuously seeking opportunities for innovation and growth. Entrepreneurship and innovation play a vital role in driving economic development, fostering competition, and promoting social change.
