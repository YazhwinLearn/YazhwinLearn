    Overview High-Rise Building Design & Analysis

    High-rise building design and analysis encompasses the planning, design, and structural analysis of tall structures. This course delves into the unique challenges and considerations involved in designing skyscrapers, including structural stability, wind resistance, vertical transportation systems, and building services. Students will explore advanced concepts in structural engineering and architectural design to create innovative and sustainable high-rise buildings.
