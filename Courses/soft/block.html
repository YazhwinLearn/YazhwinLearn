    Overview Blockchain

    Full Stack Web Development Blockchain is a decentralized ledger technology that stores data across a network of computers, ensuring transparency and security through cryptographic techniques. It enables peer-to-peer transactions without the need for intermediaries, reducing costs and increasing efficiency. Each block in the chain contains a timestamped batch of transactions, linked to the previous block, creating a chronological and immutable record of data. Consensus mechanisms like proof of work or proof of stake validate and add new blocks to the chain, maintaining integrity. Blockchain technology finds applications in various fields, including finance, supply chain management, and healthcare, revolutionizing how transactions are conducted and data is managed.
