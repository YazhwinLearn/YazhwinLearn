    Overview Aerospace Materials and Structures

    Aerospace Materials and Structures course covers the materials science and structural engineering principles essential for aerospace applications. Participants will learn about material properties, stress analysis, fatigue, and fracture mechanics. Through laboratory experiments and design projects, students will explore the selection, testing, and optimization of materials and structures for aerospace systems.
