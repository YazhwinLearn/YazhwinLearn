    Overview

    Aerospace Systems Engineering course provides insights into the interdisciplinary approach to designing and managing complex aerospace systems. Participants will learn about systems engineering processes, requirements analysis, and integration. Through case studies and collaborative projects, students will develop skills in system architecture design, verification, and validation.
