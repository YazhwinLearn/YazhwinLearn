    Overview

    Astronautics and Space Exploration course explores the science and technology behind space exploration and human spaceflight. Participants will learn about space missions, orbital mechanics, space habitats, and extraterrestrial exploration. Through simulations and projects, students will gain practical experience in mission planning, space vehicle design, and space environment analysis.
