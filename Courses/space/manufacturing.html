    Overview

    Aerospace Manufacturing and Production course covers the manufacturing processes and production techniques used in the aerospace industry. Participants will learn about additive manufacturing, CNC machining, composite fabrication, and quality control. Through hands-on projects and factory tours, students will gain insights into aerospace manufacturing operations and supply chain management.
