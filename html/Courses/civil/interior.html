OVERVIEW OF BIM

Interior design is the art and science of enhancing interior spaces to achieve a more aesthetically pleasing and functional environment. It involves creatively utilizing space, color, texture, furniture, and accessories to create harmonious and inviting interiors. Interior designers consider the needs and preferences of clients while incorporating design principles to create spaces that are both practical and visually appealing. Their expertise extends to residential, commercial, and institutional settings, ensuring that spaces are not only beautiful but also conducive to their intended purpose. Through thoughtful planning and attention to detail, interior design transforms spaces into personalized and inspiring environments.
