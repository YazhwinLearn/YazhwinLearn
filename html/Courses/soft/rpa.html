OVERVIEW RPA

Robotic Process AutomationRobotic Process Automation (RPA) is a technology that uses software robots or "bots" to automate repetitive tasks, mimicking human interactions with digital systems. RPA increases efficiency, accuracy, and scalability by executing rule-based processes without human intervention. It integrates with existing systems, operates 24/7, and can handle a variety of tasks across different applications and platforms. RPA implementations often result in cost savings, improved productivity, and enhanced customer experiences. However, successful deployment requires careful planning, monitoring, and continuous optimization to maximize its benefits.
