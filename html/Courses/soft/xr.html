OVERVIEW Extended Reality

Extended Reality (XR) is a spectrum of technologies that merge real and virtual worlds, including augmented reality (AR), virtual reality (VR), and mixed reality (MR). Through XR, users can interact with digital environments or objects superimposed onto the physical world or entirely immersive virtual spaces. XR applications range from entertainment and gaming to education, healthcare, and training, offering innovative ways to visualize information, simulate scenarios, and enhance human experiences. As XR continues to evolve, it holds immense potential to reshape industries, foster collaboration, and create transformative experiences that blur the boundaries between the physical and digital realms.
