    Overview Car Designer Internship

    A Car Designer Intern focuses on conceptualizing and sketching innovative vehicle designs, utilizing traditional drawing techniques and digital illustration tools. They collaborate closely with engineering and design teams to translate ideas into visually compelling concepts, considering factors such as aesthetics, functionality, and market appeal. Strong sketching abilities, creativity, and a keen eye for detail are essential for success in this internship.

    Technologies Covered

    Car Designer Internship Curriculum
    Month 1: Introduction to Automotive Design
    • Overview of automotive design principles
    • Introduction to car design software (e.g., Autodesk Alias, Rhino)
    • Fundamentals of 2D sketching and rendering
    • Understanding vehicle packaging and ergonomics
    Month 2: Advanced Car Design Techniques
    • Advanced modeling techniques for automotive surfaces
    • Exploring aerodynamics and vehicle performance
    • Conceptualizing and refining car designs
    • Introduction to automotive engineering fundamentals
    Month 3: Car Design Project Implementation
    • Developing comprehensive car design concepts
    • Implementing design feedback and iteration
    • Finalizing car design prototypes
    • Building a professional car design portfolio
