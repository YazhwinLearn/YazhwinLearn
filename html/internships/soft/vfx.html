Overview VFX Internship

A VFX Intern is responsible for learning and mastering visual effects techniques to enhance video content. They work on creating realistic effects such as explosions, CGI, and compositing. VFX Interns collaborate with creative teams to produce stunning visual effects and contribute to project success. Strong problem-solving, critical thinking, and creativity are essential for excelling in this internship.

Technologies Covered

VFX Internship Curriculum
Month 1: Introduction to Visual Effects
  • Understanding the principles of visual effects
  • Introduction to VFX software and tools
  • Basic compositing and layering techniques
  • Green screen and chroma keying
Month 2: Advanced VFX Techniques
  • CGI integration and 3D modeling
  • Particle simulation and dynamics
  • Motion tracking and match moving
  • Advanced compositing and matte painting
Month 3: Project Development
  • Creating VFX shots for film, TV, or advertising
  • Integration with live-action footage
  • Collaboration with directors and VFX supervisors
  • Building a professional VFX portfolio
