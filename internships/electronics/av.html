    Overview Autonomous Vehicle Internships

    An Autonomous Vehicle Intern delves into the cutting-edge technologies driving the future of transportation. They explore perception systems, artificial intelligence, robotics, and computer vision to develop autonomous vehicle solutions. Strong analytical skills, programming expertise, and a passion for innovation are key for thriving in this internship.

    Technologies Covered

    Automotive Design Internship Curriculum
    Month 1: Fundamentals of Automotive Design
    • Overview of automotive design history and trends
    • Understanding automotive design principles and aesthetics
    • Introduction to automotive design software tools
    • Sketching and rendering techniques
    Month 2: Advanced Automotive Design Techniques
    • Advanced modeling techniques for exterior and interior design
    • Surface modeling and curvature analysis
    • Integration of design aesthetics with functionality
    • Virtual prototyping and simulation
    Month 3: Automotive Design Project Implementation
    • Developing automotive design concepts and proposals
    • Implementing automotive design for prototyping and testing
    • Evaluating design performance and user feedback
    • Creating a professional automotive design portfolio
