    Overview Electric Vehicle Design & Development Internship

    An Electric Vehicle Design & Development Intern immerses themselves in the world of sustainable transportation. They contribute to the design, development, and testing of electric vehicles, focusing on areas such as battery technology, powertrain design, and energy management systems. Strong engineering skills, creativity, and a passion for environmental sustainability are essential for excelling in this internship.

    Technologies Covered

    Electric Vehicle Design & Development Internship Curriculum
    Month 1: Introduction to Electric Vehicles
    • Understanding the fundamentals of electric vehicle technology
    • Exploring different types of electric vehicles and architectures
    • Introduction to electric powertrain components and systems
    • Overview of battery technologies and charging infrastructure
    Month 2: Electric Vehicle Design Principles
    • Designing electric vehicle chassis and body structures
    • Integration of electric powertrain components into vehicle design
    • Optimizing aerodynamics and energy efficiency
    • Ensuring safety and crashworthiness of electric vehicles
    Month 3: Electric Vehicle Prototype Development
    • Building and testing electric vehicle prototypes
    • Conducting performance and reliability testing
    • Implementing feedback for design optimization
    • Preparing documentation and presentation of electric vehicle projects
