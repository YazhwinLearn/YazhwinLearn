    Overview VLSI Design Internship

    A VLSI Design Intern explores the world of very-large-scale integration (VLSI) and semiconductor design. They delve into the design and optimization of integrated circuits (ICs) for various electronic devices. Strong knowledge of digital design principles, proficiency in EDA tools, and an understanding of semiconductor physics are crucial for excelling in this internship.

    Technologies Covered

    VLSI Design Internship Curriculum
    Month 1: Introduction to VLSI Design
    • Understanding VLSI technology and design flow
    • Exploring digital logic design and RTL coding
    • Introduction to hardware description languages (Verilog, VHDL)
    • Hands-on experience with EDA tools for VLSI design
    Month 2: VLSI Circuit Design and Verification
    • Designing CMOS logic gates and sequential circuits
    • Verification techniques for functional and timing analysis
    • Introduction to VLSI layout design and physical synthesis
    • Simulating and validating VLSI circuits
    Month 3: VLSI Project Development
    • Designing and implementing VLSI projects
    • Testing and validating VLSI chip functionality
    • Documentation and presentation of VLSI projects
    • Exploring career opportunities in VLSI design and semiconductor industry
