    Overview Electric Vehicle Design Internship

    An Electric Vehicle Design Intern specializes in the design and development of electric vehicles (EVs) and their components. They contribute to the creation of EV prototypes, focusing on optimizing battery systems, powertrains, and overall vehicle performance. Knowledge of electric vehicle technology, sustainability principles, and proficiency in design software are crucial for success in this internship.

    Electric Vehicle Design Internship Curriculum
    Month 1: Introduction to Electric Vehicle Design
    • Overview of electric vehicle (EV) components and systems
    • Understanding EV architecture and powertrain design
    • Introduction to EV design software tools
    • Electric vehicle safety and regulatory considerations
    Month 2: Advanced Electric Vehicle Design Techniques
    • Advanced modeling techniques for EV components and assemblies
    • Integration of electric drivetrains with chassis and body structures
    • Battery pack design and thermal management
    • Electric vehicle performance analysis and optimization
    Month 3: Electric Vehicle Design Project Implementation
    • Developing electric vehicle design concepts and proposals
    • Implementing EV design for prototyping and testing
    • Evaluating EV performance and efficiency
    • Creating a professional electric vehicle design portfolio
