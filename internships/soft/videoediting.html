    Overview Video/Audio Editing Internship

    A Video/Audio Editing Intern is responsible for learning and mastering video and audio editing software to create compelling multimedia content. They work on editing raw footage, adding effects, and enhancing audio quality. Video/Audio Editing Interns collaborate with creative teams to produce high-quality videos and audio content. Strong problem-solving, critical thinking, and creativity are essential for excelling in this internship.

    Technologies Covered

    Video/Audio Editing Internship Curriculum
    Month 1: Introduction to Video/Audio Editing
    • Overview of video/audio editing software
    • Understanding the editing workspace
    • Basic editing techniques and tools
    • Introduction to audio mixing and effects
    Month 2: Advanced Editing Techniques
    • Color correction and grading
    • Advanced video transitions and effects
    • Audio synchronization and sound design
    • Multi-camera editing
    Month 3: Project Development
    • Editing short films, documentaries, or music videos
    • Creating promotional videos or podcasts
    • Collaboration with directors and producers
    • Building a professional editing portfolio
