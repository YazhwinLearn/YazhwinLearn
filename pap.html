What is Pay After Placement ?

Pay after placement is an innovative and student-friendly approach in education and training programs where learners are required to pay the course fees only after they secure a job related to the skills they've acquired during the training. This model is designed to reduce the financial burden on students and aligns the cost of education with the tangible outcome of obtaining employment. It reflects a commitment from educational institutions or training providers to the success of their students,emphasizing a practical and results-driven approach to learning.

How it Works

1. Low Initial Investment: Enroll in our Pay After Placement courses with a minimal upfront payment of just INR 10,000. This nominal fee covers your initial registration and secures your spot in our program.
2. Intensive Training Program: Dive into our comprehensive and industry-focused training program. Benefit from hands-on learning, real-world projects, and guidance from seasoned professionals. Acquire the skills employers are looking for in today's competitive job market.
3. Job Placement Assistance: Upon successful completion of your course, our dedicated placement team steps in. We leverage our vast network of industry connections to help you secure a job that aligns with your newly acquired skills and expertise.
4.Land Your Dream Job: Once you've secured a job, you can focus on your career growth without the burden of hefty educational loans. The remaining course fee of INR 1,40,000 is payable in convenient installments, ensuring that your financial commitment aligns with your professional success.
